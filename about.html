How It All Began

A Stitch in Time

It started in 2021 with a single skein of blush-pink merino wool and a vintage crochet hook. Our founder, Tamana Nijrabi, had just moved to a sunny India apartment and needed something to keep her hands busy and her spirit warm during long summer evenings.

What began as a personal hobby quickly became an obsession. Friends started asking where they could buy the delicate bags and boho totes she was making. An Etsy shop followed, then a waitlist, then a small studio and Cozy Threads was born.

Today, we work with a collective of 12 passionate artisans across the India, Turkey and Canada each bringing their own stitching style and cultural flair to our growing collection.

What We Believe

Our Mission & Values

Every decision we make from the yarns we source to the artisans we partner with is guided by these core values.

Handmade Always

We believe in the irreplaceable beauty of something made by hand. Every stitch is placed with intention, care and artisanal pride.

Sustainability First

We source only organic, ethically-produced natural fibres — wool, cotton and plant-based yarns that are as kind to the earth as they are to your skin.

Fair Artisan Pay

Our artisans are paid fairly and transparently. We believe the people who pour their heart into every piece deserve to be valued accordingly.

Timeless Quality

We design pieces to last years, not seasons. Slow fashion means creating things worth keeping and worth passing on.

Craftsmanship

Where Art Meets
Impeccable Craft

Every product in our collection is made using traditional crochet techniques passed down through generations elevated with modern design sensibility. We obsess over every detail so you never have to.

The Makers

Meet Our Artisans

The talented, passionate creators behind every Cozy Threads piece. They put their heart and soul into each stitch.

Tamana Nijrabi

Founder & Lead Designer

A former textile artist , Tamana started Cozy Threads to make artisan crochet accessible to everyone. She leads design and sources all our yarns personally.

Lailuma

Senior Crochet Artisan

Based in Ankara, Lailuma brings the warmth of Afghani craft traditions to every piece she creates. She specialises in intricate lacework and our beloved throw blanket collection.

Lida Ahmady

Accessories & Bag Specialist

Hailing from Canada, Lida's bold, editorial eye shapes our bags and accessories line. She's obsessed with texture and structure and her Boho Mini Bag is our forever best seller.
